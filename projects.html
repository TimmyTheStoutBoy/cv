DEVANCER

Comments: The idea of the project is the resource that would unify and centralize on one platform the resources for learning programming for all levels and needs: from amateur to professional or managerial needs. As a self-taught, most of the time I had to spend hours that at the end would turn into days, searching for the most comprehensive and "right" material, which is often referenced on stackoverflow or somewhere else by those who already went through it. I want people to save that time and go straight to the source they are seeking for. Learning programming is often portrayed as something that requires immense capabilities, while in reality it is as simple as having the will, computer and the right resources. In short, DEVANCER should make learning and keeping up with programming as easy as it should be, given the abundance of the resources and their accessibility.

Progress: The overall progress is about 70%, which includes authentication, registration, database structures, real time communication, first pages user encounters while not registered, library tools (view/create/edit resource). The real time communication was established with socket.io. The work that is left includes: admin management tools, calendar synchronization, design and code refinement, implementation of forum and blog APIs.
